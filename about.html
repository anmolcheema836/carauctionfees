About Us

Car Auction Fees UK was built to bring transparency to the confusing world of vehicle auction invoices.

Buying a car at auction can be lucrative, but the "Hammer Price" is rarely what you pay. With buyer fees, online fees, assurance levies, V5 fees, and VAT on top of fees (and sometimes on the car itself), the final bill can be a shock.

We built this tool to help private buyers and traders quickly estimate their total exposure before placing a bid.

Note: We are an independent entity and are not affiliated with any auction house.
