Copart UK Buying Guide

⚠️ The 10% Relist Warning

If you win a vehicle on Copart and fail to pay, you will be charged 10% of your bid (minimum £250) as a relist fee. Do not bid unless you have the funds ready.

Understanding "Member" vs "Non-Member"

Copart fees vary heavily based on whether you are a registered trade member or a public buyer. Public buyers often face "Internet Bid Fees" which scale with the price of the vehicle.

Gate Fees & Loading

Copart charges a "Gate Fee" (approx £50) for the physical movement of the car out of their yard. If you need them to lift the car onto a trailer (which you usually do for salvage), there is often a loading fee.
