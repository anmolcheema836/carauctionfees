BCA Buyer Fees Explained

Buying from British Car Auctions? The hammer price is just the beginning.

The "Standard Rate" Trap

Most private buyers fall into the "Standard" buyer tier. This tier pays significantly higher fees than "MyBCA" cardholders (dealers). If you are buying a car for £1,000, expect fees to approach 30-40% of the car's value once VAT is added.

Hidden Costs

  • BCA Assured (£45+): A mandatory mechanical report fee on most vehicles under 8 years old.
  • V5 Processing (£25): A standard fee for handling the logbook transfer.
  • Online Fee (£40+): If you bid online (which most do), this is added automatically.
